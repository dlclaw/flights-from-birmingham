About This Route

Flying from Birmingham to Amsterdam is one of the most popular routes from Birmingham Airport. Amsterdam Schiphol Airport (AMS) is a major European hub, making it perfect for both city breaks and connecting flights worldwide.

Flight Information

The Birmingham to Amsterdam route is well-served with multiple daily flights, offering great flexibility for travelers. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, you'll find convenient departure times throughout the day.

Flight Duration

Direct flights from Birmingham to Amsterdam typically take around 1 hour 20 minutes. This short flight time makes Amsterdam perfect for a weekend getaway or business trip.

Airlines Operating This Route

Several airlines fly direct from Birmingham to Amsterdam:

  • KLM Royal Dutch Airlines: The flagship carrier offers multiple daily flights with excellent service
  • Budget Airlines: Low-cost carriers often provide competitive fares, especially when booked in advance

When to Book

To get the best deals on Birmingham to Amsterdam flights:

  • Book 2-3 months in advance for the lowest fares
  • Tuesday and Wednesday are typically the cheapest days to fly
  • Avoid weekends when prices tend to be higher
  • Off-season (Nov-Mar) excluding holidays offers the best prices

What to Expect in Amsterdam

Amsterdam is one of Europe's most charming cities, famous for its canals, museums, and vibrant culture.

Top Attractions

  • Canal Cruise: See the city from its iconic waterways
  • Van Gogh Museum: World's largest collection of Van Gogh's works
  • Anne Frank House: Moving historical museum
  • Rijksmuseum: Dutch art and history masterpieces
  • Jordaan District: Trendy neighborhood with boutiques and cafes

Getting Around Amsterdam

Amsterdam has excellent public transport:

  • Train from Airport: Direct trains to Central Station (15-20 minutes)
  • Trams: Extensive network covering the city
  • Bikes: The most Dutch way to explore - rental shops everywhere
  • Walking: The city center is very walkable

Travel Tips

  • Amsterdam uses the Euro (‚Ç¨) - card payments widely accepted
  • English is widely spoken throughout the city
  • Book museums in advance to skip queues
  • The OV-chipkaart is the easiest way to use public transport
  • Try traditional Dutch pancakes and stroopwafels
  • Schiphol Airport has excellent shopping and dining if you have time

Visa Requirements

UK passport holders can visit the Netherlands for tourism or business for up to 90 days within a 180-day period without a visa. Ensure your passport has at least 6 months validity from your travel date.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For the best prices, book 6-8 weeks before departure. However, deals can appear at any time, so it's worth monitoring prices.

Are there direct flights?

Yes, Birmingham to Amsterdam has multiple direct flights daily, making it very convenient.

What's the cheapest month to fly?

November through February (excluding Christmas/New Year) typically offers the lowest fares, though weather can be cold and wet.

Can I take hand luggage only?

Yes, all airlines allow hand luggage. This is a short flight, so many travelers go cabin-baggage-only to save time and money.
