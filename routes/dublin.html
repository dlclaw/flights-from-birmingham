Why Fly Birmingham to Dublin?

Dublin is one of the closest international cities to Birmingham, with flights taking just over an hour. This short hop makes it perfect for weekend breaks, business trips, or connecting to North America via Dublin's pre-clearance facility.

Discovering Dublin

Dublin blends historic charm with modern vibrancy. From medieval castles to bustling Temple Bar, there's something for everyone.

Must-See Attractions

  • Guinness Storehouse: Learn about Ireland's famous stout and enjoy rooftop views
  • Trinity College & Book of Kells: Historic university and stunning illuminated manuscript
  • Temple Bar: Lively district with pubs, restaurants, and street performers
  • Dublin Castle: 800 years of history in the heart of the city
  • St. Patrick's Cathedral: Ireland's largest cathedral
  • Kilmainham Gaol: Former prison, now a fascinating museum

Dublin Food & Drink

Don't miss authentic Irish experiences:

  • Traditional Irish breakfast at a local cafe
  • Pint of Guinness in a historic pub
  • Fresh seafood at Howth fishing village

Day Trips from Dublin

  • Cliffs of Moher: Spectacular coastal cliffs (2.5 hours)
  • Glendalough: Ancient monastic site in stunning valley (1 hour)

  • Newgrange: 5,000-year-old passage tomb (1 hour)
