Birmingham Airport Guide

Everything you need to know about flying from BHX

About Birmingham Airport

Birmingham Airport (BHX) is the UK's seventh-busiest airport and the third-busiest outside London. Located in Solihull, West Midlands, it serves over 13 million passengers annually and offers flights to more than 150 destinations worldwide.

Getting to Birmingham Airport

By Train

Birmingham International railway station is directly connected to the airport via the free Air-Rail Link monorail (90-second journey). The station has frequent services from:

  • Birmingham New Street: 10-15 minutes
  • London Euston: 1 hour 10 minutes
  • Manchester: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Coventry: 10 minutes

By Car

The airport is easily accessible from the M42 motorway (Junction 6) and A45. Clear signposting makes it straightforward to reach from anywhere in the Midlands.

Parking

Multiple parking options are available:

  • Short Stay: Ideal for dropping off or picking up (2-5 minutes walk to terminal)
  • Long Stay: Budget-friendly for longer trips (free shuttle bus to terminal)
  • Meet & Greet: Drop your car at the terminal, we park it for you
  • Off-Airport: Cheapest option with shuttle transfers

Terminal Facilities

Birmingham Airport operates from a single terminal building, making connections and navigation simple.

Dining & Shopping

The terminal offers extensive dining and shopping options:

  • Over 60 shops including duty-free
  • 30+ restaurants, cafes, and bars
  • High-street brands and luxury retailers
  • Grab-and-go options for quick bites

Airport Lounges

Several lounges are available for a more comfortable pre-flight experience:

  • No. 1 Lounge: Open to all passengers (pay on arrival or pre-book)
  • Aspire Lounge: Premium lounge with excellent facilities
  • Airline Lounges: Available for business and first-class passengers

Check-In & Security

Check-In Times

  • Short-haul flights: Check-in opens 2-3 hours before departure
  • Long-haul flights: Check-in opens 3-4 hours before departure
  • Online check-in: Available 24 hours before most flights

Security Screening

Birmingham Airport is introducing new security technology to speed up the screening process. Currently:

  • Arrive at least 2 hours before short-haul flights
  • Arrive at least 3 hours before long-haul flights
  • Peak times (early morning and evenings) may have longer queues
  • Fast Track security is available for purchase

Popular Airlines at Birmingham

Major carriers operating from BHX include:

  • Ryanair: Extensive European network
  • easyJet: Popular European destinations
  • Jet2: Holiday destinations and city breaks
  • TUI: Package holidays
  • Emirates: Dubai and beyond
  • KLM: Amsterdam and worldwide connections
  • Lufthansa: German cities and global network

Destinations from Birmingham

Birmingham Airport connects you to over 150 destinations across:

  • Europe: Spain, France, Italy, Greece, Portugal, and more
  • North America: New York, Orlando, Las Vegas (seasonal)
  • Middle East: Dubai, Doha, Abu Dhabi
  • Asia: Singapore, Bangkok (via connections)
  • Caribbean: Barbados, Jamaica (seasonal)

Travel Tips

  • Book parking in advance for the best rates (up to 60% cheaper)
  • Use online check-in to save time at the airport
  • Download your airline's app for real-time updates
  • Check your passport has at least 6 months validity for international travel
  • Pack liquids in a clear bag for security (100ml max per item)
  • Sign up for airline loyalty programs to earn points and benefits
