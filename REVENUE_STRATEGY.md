## Maximizing Affiliate Revenue

### Optimize Conversion Rate

**Best practices:**

1. **Make prices prominent**
   - Large, bold price display
   - Green color = savings
   - "Great Deal" badges

2. **Create urgency**
   - "Updated 2h ago"
   - "Price may change"
   - Limited availability messaging

3. **Build trust**
   - Show real prices upfront
   - No bait-and-switch
   - Clear affiliate disclosure

4. **Reduce friction**
   - One-click to booking
   - Affiliate ID in all links
   - Fast page load

5. **Mobile optimization**
   - Most traffic is mobile
   - Easy thumb-friendly buttons
   - Fast loading on 3G
